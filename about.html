OUR STORY

Imagine a place where every day people eat, sleep and dream about ice cream… That’s Chilly Cones. It all started over 75 years ago, when everyone else settled for vanilla, chocolate and strawberry, Chilly Roberts and Dan Cones had the crazy idea of offering 31 flavors of ice cream – one for every day of the month. Burt and Irv were absolutely nuts about ice cream (Irv even had an ice-cream-cone-shaped swimming pool). Their passion was contagious, and we like to think our 1300+ flavors have made the world a yummier place since opening back in 1923.
